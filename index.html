The team behind j.E.P.H.

Hailing from the each of the three corners of the earth, which was recently confirmed to be triangular (it makes sense, seeing how it is the strongest mathematical shape), two young men and one less so joined forces to create arguably the most sophisticated work of art in modern times.

These three men–henceforth referred to as the Pale Council–put forth a herculean effort over a fortnight, culminating in the very pages you are currently having the pleasure of experiencing.

What can the Pale Council accomplish? Perhaps the better question would be "what can they not accomplish?". Amongst themselves they possess unfathomable knowledge about nigh every conceivable subject.

I implore any reader who doubts the legitimacy of this claim to scour the trichiliocosm in its entirety for greater minds; you will not succeed. To the right the reader will find chronicles of their recent exploits as well as their grandest undertakings to peruse at their leisure.
