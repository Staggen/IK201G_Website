These three men–henceforth referred to as the Pale Council–put forth a herculean effort over a fortnight, culminating in the very page you are currently having the pleasure of experiencing.

What can the Pale Council accomplish? Perhaps the better question would be "what can they not accomplish?". Amongst themselves they possess unfathomable knowledge about nigh every conceivable subject.
